An arcade stick and more buttons based on my previous work. The new buttons are based on a faux Nintendo 64 style arcade cabinet. Each button fits inside 32 pixels squared or less, and has a second frame of animation of the button being pressed.

This is an expansion of my original pixel-art arcade buttons.

Sprites used in the example are from Open Gunner Starter Kit

Inspired by and based on Control Icons and Gerald's Keys

Uses DawnBringer's 16 colour palette
